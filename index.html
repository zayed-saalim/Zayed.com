Journey

1992

Born in Botswana

Born in Botswana and later moved to Plymouth, England in 1994, shaping an early drive for independence and entrepreneurship.

2014

Co-Founds Social Chain

Dropped out of university to launch Social Chain, building it into one of the world's most influential social media marketing agencies before stepping down as co-CEO in 2020.

2017

Launches 'The Diary of a CEO'

Created the acclaimed podcast series, which has since grown into one of the largest podcasts globally, featuring deep-dive interviews with world leaders, thinkers, and entrepreneurs.

2021

Flightstory, Dragons' Den & Books

Co-founded Flightstory and thirdweb. Became the youngest-ever Dragon on BBC's Dragons' Den. Published the Sunday Times bestseller 'Happy Sexy Millionaire'.

2025/2026

Global Empire & Valuation

Named among Forbes World Top 10 Creators. Steven.com hits a $425M valuation, securing major funding to build the 'Disney of the Creator Economy'. Continues scaling an expansive investment portfolio including Huel, Zoe, and PerfectTed.
