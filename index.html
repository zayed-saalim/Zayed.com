About Me

My Journey & Vision

Born in 2012, I am a young entrepreneur driven by a passion for technology, business, and innovation. As a practicing Muslim, my faith guides every step I take, ensuring that my work aligns with Islamic principles of honesty, fairness, and social responsibility.

Currently studying at Baseerah International School, Dhaka (BISD), I balance academic growth with entrepreneurial ambition. I actively build and manage e-commerce ventures, focusing on ethical business practices while exploring global markets through dropshipping and digital solutions.

With intermediate-level development skills and a strong interest in technology, I create tools and systems that serve both business and community needs, blending creativity, practicality, and faith-driven purpose.

Core Principles

Ethical Entrepreneurship: I believe that business success should never come at the expense of integrity. My operations prioritize fair dealings, honest marketing, and customer satisfaction above all.

Continuous Learning: Balancing school with entrepreneurship has taught me the value of lifelong learning. I'm constantly improving both my business strategies and technical development skills.

Tech-Driven Solutions: As a developer, I leverage technology to create efficient, user-friendly solutions that enhance the customer experience and streamline business operations.

Community Impact: I measure success not just by profit, but by positive impact—creating value for customers, supporting ethical supply chains, and inspiring other young entrepreneurs.

Challenging Conventional Education: I believe traditional schools often focus more on training students to follow orders than to think critically or innovate. My approach emphasizes self-directed learning, practical skills, and developing real-world problem-solving abilities that prepare me for entrepreneurship and technological innovation.

  • Continuous Innovation: Leveraging technology to improve services and operations.
  • Social Responsibility: Ensuring business activities contribute positively to community wellbeing.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Writing books to share insights and help other young entrepreneurs succeed.
